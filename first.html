Accessories

Gloves, Lifting straps, belts, bar pads and Bags these are some of the accessories you'll see people bring to the gym. These are all great but realize that these are assisting at a point where you are week, It's always important to have a day at the gym where you work those weak points. My day is Sunday morning when I'm the only one there!

Gym Etiquette

Here's the easiest thing to accomplish yet you'll only see it once and an while. Simple things like rememberong to rack your weights or cleaning of the equipment after you sweat on it. I know it seems gross and you don't know how someone can do that, I chalk it up to "forgetting" because they are lifting so hard that their brain is shutting down. It helps with my sanity or whatevers left! Remember treat the gym like your house keep that shit clean so your not emabrressed when someone shows up unannounced.
